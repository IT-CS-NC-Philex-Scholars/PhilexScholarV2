# Student User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Student Portal to apply for scholarships and manage your requirements.

## 1. Getting Started

### Profile Setup
Upon logging in for the first time, you must complete your profile.
-   **Navigate to**: Profile (Sidebar)
-   **Required Info**: Address, School Type (High School/College), School Name, and Level.
-   **Why?**: Scholarships are hidden until you define your school type. The system matches you only with programs you are eligible for.

## 2. Applying for Scholarships

1.  **Browse**: Go to the **Scholarships** tab. You will see cards for available programs.
2.  **View Details**: Click a card to see requirements, deadlines, and benefits.
3.  **Apply**: Click "Apply Now". This creates a **Draft**.
4.  **Upload Documents**:
    -   You will be asked to upload specific files (e.g., Report Card, ID).
    -   Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG.
    -   *Tip*: Ensure images are clear and readable.
5.  **Submit**: Once all documents are uploaded, click **Submit Application**.

## 3. Tracking Your Application

Check your **Dashboard** for status updates.
-   **Documents Pending**: You need to upload more files.
-   **Under Review**: Admin is checking your documents.
-   **Documents Rejected**: One or more files were invalid. Check the specific feedback and upload a new one.
-   **Enrolled**: Congratulations! You have been accepted.

## 4. Community Service

Once enrolled, you are required to complete community service hours before receiving funds.

### The Dashboard
Navigate to **Community Service** in your application view. You have two ways to report:

### Option A: Time Tracking (Real-time)
1.  **Clock In**: Click "Start Entry" when you begin your service.
2.  **Perform Service**: Do your work.
3.  **Clock Out**: Click "End Entry".
4.  **Details**: Enter a description of what you did, what you learned, and upload photos as proof.

### Option B: Quick Report / PDF Upload
If you have a certificate or a signed paper from an organization:
1.  Select **"Upload Report"**.
2.  Enter the total hours/days.
3.  Upload the PDF evidence.

### Progress
A progress bar will show your completion status (e.g., "4/6 Days Completed"). Once you hit the target, your status moves to **Service Completed**.

## 5. Disbursement

After your community service is approved, the status changes to **Disbursement Pending**.
-   You don't need to do anything here.
-   Wait for the admin to process the payment.
-   Status will update to **Disbursed** when funds are released.
